American Shorthair

American Shorthair cats are loving, but don’t let this fool you! American Shorthair cats are outstanding hunters, and were first brought to North America by European settlers for keeping the pest population in control. As a widely popular cat breed, the American Shorthair cat has been selectively bred since the early 1900s to retain their personality.
